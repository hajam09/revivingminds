ABOUT US

RevivingMinds  is about studying human thoughts & behaviour, and developing concepts & practice towards holistic well-being of individuals, families and societies, thereby building healthier inidividuals and stronger communities. RevivingMinds  offer consultations, therapies and support groups for all physical & psycho-social stress and problems at home, work or at any situation, taking into consideration the spiritual & cultural values.
